About Us

FreddyCode Academy was founded with one goal: to make learning web development simple, practical, and enjoyable.

Rather than focusing only on theory, our training emphasizes hands-on experience. Every lesson is followed by practical exercises and projects that help you understand how websites are built in the real world.

We believe that learning by doing is the fastest path to becoming a confident developer. Throughout the program, you'll receive guidance, feedback, and encouragement as you grow your skills.

Our Mission

To empower beginners with practical web development skills that prepare them to create modern websites, solve real-world problems, and begin successful careers in technology.

Our Vision

To build a community of passionate developers who continuously learn, innovate, and inspire others through technology.

Yes. Assignments are an important part of the learning process and help reinforce each lesson.
